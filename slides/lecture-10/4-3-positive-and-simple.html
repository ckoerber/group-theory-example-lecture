IV The highest-weight construction scheme

IV.3 Positive weights and simple roots

Motivation

So far we have seen how to construct many sub $su(2)$s for general simple Lie algebras. But how can we use those to find explicit representations or bases?

  • Is it possible to know how many roots are present in a general algebra?
  • Do we need all of the roots to identify all states in a representation?
  • What is the notion of positiveness for root vectors?
    (i.e., what does it mean to apply raising and lowering operations in a multi-dimensional space?)
  • Does the order of applying multiple creation and annihilation operators matter?
    And if so, how do we find the right orders?
